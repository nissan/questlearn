## ⚠️ Common Pitfalls

- **Don't move cursor frantically** — smooth, deliberate movements only
- **Wait for loading** — QuestLearn needs 3–5 sec for AI responses
- **Don't close tabs** — keep it minimal throughout
- **No typos** — if you typo, backspace and retype before pressing Enter
- **Don't talk** — audio is added in post from voiceover file
