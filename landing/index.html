QuestLearn

AI-powered learning that meets students
where their curiosity already lives.

A Year 9 student stuck on photosynthesis doesn't open a textbook. They pick a format — a meme, a story, a puzzle, a game, or a short film — and our AI generates curriculum-aligned content in that format, instantly.

Then it asks them questions back. Not to test them. To make them think.

A student in Bourke gets the same experience as a student in Bondi. A remote teacher finally sees which topics their class is struggling with — not at report card time, but right now.

Design principles

🙅

No leaderboards. Ever.

Personal progress only. Competitive mechanics harm the students who need help most.

😄

Wrong answers get memes, not buzzers

Encouragement + explanation on every wrong answer. Never a fail state.

🧭

Student agency is the feature

We never assign a format. The student always chooses. Autonomy drives engagement.

🔒

Privacy by design

No persistent student data. Teacher sees aggregated heatmap only — never individual surveillance.
